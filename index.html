    • History of the Katana

    Katanas are swords made by the Japanese mainly used in battle. These swords have characteristics that stand out from other swords, it has a curved blade, a square or circular guard, and a long grip so that the user can hold it with two hands. Katanas were used by samurai and the military noblility of fuedal Japan up to the Edo period. During these times many users of the katana invented their own martial art with the katana. Prime examples or these arts would be Kendo and Kenjutsu. Their was also another art made by a legendary swordsmen who was the first to use a duel wieling art, this art is called Hyoho Niten Ichi-ryu or Two Heavens as One. This art would allow the user to battle with a katana in one hand and a wakizashi in the other, the katana was defense while the wakizashi was offense. The art was created by the legendary swordsmen Miyamoto Musashi. The age of katanas soon ended after his death in the year 1868.
